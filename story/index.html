Who's Eric?
  One Friday evening in late November 2015, we were blessed with a notification on eHarmony. We arrived into each other's inboxes and landed into each other's lives. After talking for a little less than a week, we had our first date, and it was as if we already knew each other. Conversation flowed easily and our relationship began.
  In a little over a year, we have experienced so much together and most of it has been with you all! Dances, concerts, road trips, births, deaths, smiles, and tears. In a year, we have seen each other at highs and lows. Between the extremes, we have experienced everyday life together.
  With this all in mind, we are thrilled to entering into marriage together. This is a union that we do not take lightly. We are excited for you to join with us as we commit to loving and serving each other for the rest of our lives.
